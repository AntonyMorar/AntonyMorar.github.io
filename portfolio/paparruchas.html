Paparuchas

A funny puzzle game about importance of digital self-care

About this game

There is an epidemic of fake news causing spirals in the eyes of those who read it. Help Alex and his friends solve the mystery of the Paparruchas and test your dexterity and decision-making skills in this playable fable.

Paparruchas is a funny puzzle game, in which you have to find and throw away the fake news represented by basic figures such as squares, circles, triangles, etc.

For more info: itch.io page

Role: Programmer
Programs: Godot, Procreate
Team: 4
Production: 1 Month
Status: Early concept development

Gameplay

A lot of news fall on the screen and you have to look at the top to see which ones are true. Decide whether to throw them away or give them to your friends.

Original Concept

Fall windows with various challenges. To win you have to do the dynamics correctly and so desempaparruchar the animals left. You lose if they accumulate more animals and these are added when you get the challenges wrong. If you solve correctly too you will add points and it will depend on what so well you do it.
