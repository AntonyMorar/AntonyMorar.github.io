Mini Maik

A platform puzzle game about finding answers of existence

About this game

Minimaik is a simple platform puzzle game where you have to use your robot skills (graping hook) to meet your creator again.

Role: Sole developer
Programs: Unity, Asesprite, LDTK
Production: 2 Months
Status: Shipped

Gameplay

In mini mike you have the basic skills of a platformer, move and jump. But you also have a special ability, the grappling hook, which allows you to drag charged objects (yellow). Each level challenges you to think how to get to the final door.

I paid special attention to the pillars of a good platformer. For that I paid attention to things like edge detection, coyote jump, jump buffer, Squash & Stretch animations, etc.

Level Design

The level design was made with the LDTK tool that allows you to create the maps in a faster and easier way.
