Neon Wars

Neon Wars is a game clone made in Unity, based in Geometry Wars Evolved Xbox game. the game takes place in a closed space where you are a spaceship and you have to destroy as many geometric figures as possible while appear, each figure has different abilities and ways of moving.

A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
